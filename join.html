Volunteers at SecondLife Solutions

Help us, to help an item find purpose, and a community find strength.

Why volunteer at SecondLife Solutions?

1. You’re give back to your community.
2. You’re giving a new life to a perfectly good item that would have otherwise gone to waste.
3. You’re supporting a child’s education, who wouldn’t have had the opportunity otherwise.
4. You’re developing you - by learning essential skills like organizing, communication and teamwork.
5. You’re networking - this helps you expand your resources, and get involved in other worthy causes.
6. You’re inspiring someone else to inspire change.

CONTRIBUTORS

Is your school overflowing with lost property?
Are you always looking around for a kid or institution that would make the best use of stuff you no longer need?
Do you want to give a little time for a large impact?
Would you donate a small sum of rupees for a great amount of good?
If so, join as a contributor!
As a contributor, you would have to follow a simple three step process!
1. List the items, along with their quantities, and characteristics in the categories provided.
2. Select which form of a distributor you would like for this to reach.
3. Keep the items ready and labeled for when the courier comes to pick them up from your doorstep!

DISTRIBUTORS

Are you an NGO / School working with underprivileged kids?
Are you always looking for daily items for kids who may not be able to pay for these items?
Do you have access to people who need stuff from kids and teens?
Are you an external teacher, looking for old books?
If so, join as a distributor.
As a distributor, you would have to follow a simple three step process!
1. Select the type of item you would require.
2. Select the specific items, as well as their quantity.
3. Send us your address and proof of identification; and we will have these at your doorstep!

GOOD SAMARITANS

Have a few rupees to spare after a birthday?
Are you looking to celebrate by going beyond family & friends?
Join as a Good Samaritan!
Why not offer your contribution towards boxes and shipping / courier costs by buying and booking boxes to help transport items?
As a Good Samaritan, you would have the simplest two step process.
1. Find the cause you identify with!
2. Book a courier service at the time specified to you.
