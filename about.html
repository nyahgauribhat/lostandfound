Everybody deserves a second chance. You, me, and them. That’s why we exist!

The inspiration behind “SecondLife Solutions Mumbai” emerged from our personal experiences. My older brother would walk up the stairs to his class every day, and notice a large pile of lost items, that increased by the day. He would come home and tell us about this. As 11 year olds, we began to notice it too. But what we also saw was that nothing was being done with this.
Through our lives, my mother has had a very strong passion for the environment, that has passed onto us. Seeing these items without a purpose got us thinking about the environmental consequences of these. Why was it fair, that an item that had been lost could not have another life?That it was perfectly good, but could not be used another time? The wastage just never stopped.
And then, we looked at the social aspect of this. 15 crore children in India don’t have access to stationary, clothes, basic items that we lose in those piles. So we thought, why not connect the two? Why not take this unclaimed lost property, clean it up, and send it to the NGO’s so children like us could have the same resources?
This started out with my brother leading collections at our school, every three months. As he graduated, he passed the initiative down to me.
Now, we have expanded to over 15 schools in Mumbai, and over 10 NGO's !
