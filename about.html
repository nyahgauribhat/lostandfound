Everybody deserves a second chance. You, me, and them. That’s why we exist!

The inspiration behind “SecondLife Solutions Mumbai” emerged from our personal experiences. My older brother walked up the school stairs each day, noticing a large pile of lost items, that increased by the day. As 11 year olds, we began to notice it too. My mother's strong passion for the environment passed down to us. Watching these items without a purpose led us to think of the environmental consequences of these. Why was it fair, that an item that had been lost could not have another life?That it was perfectly good, but could not be used another time? Then, looking at it from a social perpective, 15 crore children in India don’t have access to stationary, clothes, basic items that we lose in those piles. So we thought, why not connect the two? Why not take this unclaimed lost property, clean it up, and send it to the NGO’s so children like us could have the same resources? This started out with my brother leading collections at our school, every three months. As he graduated, he passed the initiative down to me. Now, we have expanded to over 15 schools in Mumbai, and over 10 NGO's!
